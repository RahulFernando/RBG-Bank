RGB Bank PLC is a member-owned financial cooperative. This means that we exist to serve the needs of the consumers who own accounts at (or shares of) RGB Bank PLC. They are our owners, and their financial well-being is at the heart of every decision we make. We are one of the largest credit unions in Sri-Lanka, and one of the most stable. Our purpose is simple: to provide a sound, convenient alternative to traditional banking.
